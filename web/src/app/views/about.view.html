Hi, we're The Kindness Project

We're a team of developers based in London, UK, on a mission to help the vulnerable amongst us get support during the current pandemic.

If you need help, please create a post and reach out to those offering help in your area.

You can help by reaching out to those who need it, spreading the word on social media, and donating to help us cover costs & expand our efforts.

If you would like to get in touch, please tweet us or email us.

We're all in this together. Stay safe!
